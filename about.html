Why Computer Science?

My initial interest in computer science came from my extended involvement in the game Runescape. I discovered the world of macroing: externally programming the game to play itself. I had no idea how it was done, or even where to begin, but I was obsessed with the concept. I started my first Java programming class in junior year with my best friend Doug, another person eager to learn about the world of software development. Admittedly, my skills in coding didn't develop as quickly as I had hoped; I was dependent on Doug to walk me through the projects, debug my programs, etc. However, to my surprise, one day it all clicked. I embraced the mindset of a coder and was finally fluent enough to begin really developing my skills. My mind was set and I knew I would want to make a career in the computer science field.

Interests
